## Key Insights from VERIFIED Pricing

### 1. Pixid's Hidden Costs

**Advertised:** 299฿ (Standard)
**Reality for full features:** 1,290฿ (Ultra) + 100฿ (LINE OA) = 1,390฿+

- AI only in Ultra-lite (expires midnight) or Ultra (1,290฿)
- LINE OA is mandatory add-on for Thai market
- FTP only in Pro tier and above
- **Hidden markup:** 4.6x the entry price

### 2. SiKram's Credit Confusion

**Advertised:** 499฿ (Hobby)
**Reality for full features:** 3,990฿ (Premium)

- Hobby packages expire in 48 hours (forces repurchase)
- Credits are confusing (1,000 vs 3,000 vs 7,000 vs 20,000)
- Premium includes all features and monthly billing
- **Hidden markup:** 8x the entry price

### 3. MangoMango's Stacking Problem

**Single event pricing works fine for small events (500 photos = 1,500฿)**
**But at scale, photographers must stack tiers**, forcing much higher spend

- Example: 10K photos/month needs multiple M/L tiers
- No monthly subscription option
- Ideal for event studios, expensive for multiple photographers

### 4. FaceLink Advantage

**Transparent, consistent, cheapest at all scales**

- No hidden add-ons
- No credit confusion
- No feature gating
- Credits roll over (no waste)
- 0.36-0.48฿ per photo regardless of volume
